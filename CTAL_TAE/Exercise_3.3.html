Exercise 3.3: Analyze TAS factors.

Learning Objective ALTA-E-3.2.4 (K4) Analyze factors of implementation, use and maintenance requirements for a given TAS.
Time 40 Minutes
Version 1.1

In Ex 3.1 you designed a TAA, revisit the design and look at the approach chosen for automating test cases. Determine which approach is most suitable in your situation:

  • Capture/playback
  • Linear scripting
  • Structured scripting
  • Data-driven testing
  • Keyword-driven testing
  • Process-driven testing
  • Model-based testing

After selecting an approach, determine how you would implement it. List the changes that need to be made. Also take into account the technical considerations.

  • Interfaces of the SUT
  • SUT data
  • SUT configurations
  • SUT standards & legal settings
  • Tools & environments used to develop the SUT
  • Test interfaces in the software product
